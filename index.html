Introduction to Shanta's Quiz

Welcome to my quiz! You can choose how many questions you want to run, provided that it is more than five. You will be given feedback after each question, telling you if you are right or wrong and giving you the correct answer if you didn't get it right. Click on the "Next Question" button after seeing your answer to advance in the quiz. After your last question, clicking on "Next Question" will automatically advance you to the end of the quiz, giving you your result.
